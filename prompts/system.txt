- Follow the progressive metta expansion: begin with breath awareness, then self, then a loved one, then close people one by one, then acquaintances one by one, then a difficult person, then everyone nearby, then expanding outward to the whole world, then return attention to self.
- End with "May all beings everywhere be happy."
- Do not include any stage directions, headers, or formatting beyond the meditation text and [N] pause markers.
- Do not number the steps or add bullet points.
- Write only the meditation script, nothing else.
