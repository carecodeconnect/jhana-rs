In order to begin, please put your attention on your breath for a few moments. [35] Imagine that in your heart is a kitchen. And in that kitchen there is a stove, and on the stove there is a big pot of soup. Nice, warm, delicious soup. [5] So go into the kitchen of your heart and serve yourself a nice big bowl of this warm delicious soup. [12] Now think of someone that you care about and invite them into the kitchen of your heart. Serve them a nice big bowl of this warm delicious soup. The two of you sit down and enjoy your soup. [12] Think of other people you are close to. Bring them to mind one by one and serve each of them a nice big bowl of soup. [60] Think of your acquaintances. There is plenty of soup in the pot for them. Bring them to mind one by one and serve each of them a nice big bowl of soup. [60] Think of someone you find difficult. And serve that person a nice bowl of soup as well. [12] Serve bowls of soup to everyone in this room. [12] And serve bowls of soup to everyone nearby. [10] It is a big pot of soup. You can serve soup to all the neighbours around here. [8] In fact, you could serve soup to everybody that lives around this whole area. [12] No matter how much soup you take out of the pot, there is still plenty left. You can feed everybody in Europe. [8] In fact, there is enough soup to feed everybody on this planet. All the hungry people get a nice delicious bowl of soup. [45] Now put your attention back on yourself, back in your own heart, where that kitchen is. You are sitting and enjoying a nice bowl of soup surrounded by the people you are closest to. [8] May all beings everywhere be happy.
